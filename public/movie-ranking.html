The best movies according to Megha

My recent top 3 movies

Kill

"Kill" is an intense thriller packed with action and numerous fight sequences. The movie keeps you on the edge of your seat with its gripping storyline and high-octane scenes.

Maharaj

"Maharaj" presents a fresh perspective on how to view the world and fight for justice. The film explores themes of righteousness and determination, showcasing the struggles and triumphs of standing up for what is right.

Wild Wild Punjab

"Wild Wild Punjab" is a light-hearted comedy romance that brings together humor and love in a delightful blend. Set against the backdrop of Punjab, the movie is sure to entertain with its amusing situations and charming characters.
